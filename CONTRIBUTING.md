# Contributing

KnowledgeCivic welcomes research notes, explanations, translations, lesson plans, local case studies, accessibility improvements, and corrections.

## Before opening a change

1. Search existing issues and content.
2. State the public question the contribution helps people answer.
3. Prefer primary, official, scholarly, or directly verifiable sources.
4. Separate facts, interpretations, uncertainty, and recommendations.
5. Check licensing. Do not paste copyrighted material in bulk; summarize it in your own words and link to the source.

## Review checklist

- Is the intended audience clear?
- Can a reader verify important claims?
- Are dates, geography, definitions, and limitations explicit?
- Does the text represent relevant disagreement fairly?
- Is it readable on a phone and usable with assistive technology?
- Does it avoid partisan campaigning and undisclosed conflicts of interest?

Small corrections are welcome. Substantive claims should receive review from at least one person who did not author the change.
