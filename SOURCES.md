# Source Register

The initial materials draw on the following authoritative public resources. The project uses them as references and writes its own summaries rather than copying them in bulk.

- UNESCO, [Recommendation on Open Science](https://www.unesco.org/en/legal-affairs/recommendation-open-science), 2021.
- UNESCO, [About Open Science](https://www.unesco.org/en/open-science/about).
- UNESCO, [Media and Information Literacy](https://www.unesco.org/en/ami).
- UNESCO, [Media and Information Literacy Curriculum and Competency Framework](https://www.unesco.org/mil4teachers/en/curriculum-framework).
- UNESCO, [Media and Information Literacy for Global Communication and Learning](https://www.unesco.org/en/media-information-literacy/global-communication-learning).
- OHCHR, [Universal Declaration of Human Rights](https://www.ohchr.org/en/UDHR/Documents/60UDHR/DECLARATION.pdf), 1948.
- OHCHR, [Human Rights Education in Primary and Secondary School Systems](https://www.ohchr.org/Documents/Publications/SelfAssessmentGuideforGovernments.pdf).
- OECD, [Building trust through education](https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/building-trust-through-education_967fa34d-en.html), 2025.
