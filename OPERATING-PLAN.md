# Initial Operating Plan

## First 12 months

### Months 1-3: Foundation

- Establish the legal and financial structure appropriate to the operating jurisdiction.
- Publish the governance, licensing, privacy, and safeguarding policies.
- Recruit a small editorial council with varied expertise and lived experience.

### Months 4-6: Pilot

- Publish 10 source-based knowledge packs.
- Run the six-session Knowledge Citizen learning path with at least two community groups.
- Record corrections, accessibility issues, and learner feedback.

### Months 7-9: Local adaptation

- Support translations and locally authored packs.
- Publish a contributor guide and reviewer training.
- Test a lightweight public issue research process.

### Months 10-12: Evaluation

- Publish an impact and transparency report.
- Measure reach, reuse, contributor diversity, correction time, and learner outcomes without collecting unnecessary personal data.
- Revise the roadmap from evidence rather than ambition alone.

## Success is not follower count

The project should be judged by whether people can find, understand, verify, adapt, and use the material responsibly.
